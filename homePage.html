KUTCH
Kutch district is a district of Gujarat state in western India, with its headquarters at Bhuj. Covering an area of 45,674 km², it is the largest district of India.
AHMEDABAD
Ahmedabad is the most populous city in the Indian state of Gujarat. It is the administrative headquarters of the Ahmedabad district and the seat of the Gujarat High Court.
VADODARA
Vadodara, also known as Baroda, is the second largest city in the Indian state of Gujarat.
JUNAGADH
Junagadh is the headquarters of Junagadh district in the Indian state of Gujarat. Located at the foot of the Girnar hills, 355 kilometres southwest of Ahmedabad and Gandhinagar, it is the seventh largest city in the state.
